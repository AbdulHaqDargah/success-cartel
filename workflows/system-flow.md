## Strategic Workflow

1. Onboarding Advisor
   - Diagnoses onboarding effectiveness
   - Identifies structural risks

2. Adoption Advisor
   - Builds on onboarding insights
   - Identifies behavioral and usage gaps

3. QBR Advisor
   - Synthesizes insights into executive narrative
   - Aligns future strategy

## Key Principle

Each agent builds on the previous one.

This creates a continuous feedback loop across the customer lifecycle.

## System Value

- Standardizes decision-making across CSMs  
- Reduces variability in execution  
- Enables scale across large account portfolios
